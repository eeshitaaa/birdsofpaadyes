FAQ

Quick answers before you buy

Will all shades show on dark hair?

Not all. Use the "Dark hair friendly" chip to view shades designed to be visible on deeper natural bases.

How long does semi-permanent color last?

Most shades in this collection last between 6 to 12 washes, depending on hair porosity and wash routine.

Why separate bleach from color jars?

It keeps the main collection aligned to user intent and reduces the chance of selecting the wrong product type during checkout.
